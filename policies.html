About Us

Welcome to our library booking platform! This website is developed and maintained by Chinmay Agrawal with the goal of offering a modern, convenient way to book study spaces online.

We aim to provide a peaceful and accessible study environment for all students and self-learners. With simple seat booking, secure online payments, and instant confirmations, your library experience is now smoother than ever.

Library Booking Policies

Pricing Policy

All seat bookings at our library are subject to the following pricing:

  • Full Day Booking: ₹800
  • Half Day (Morning) Booking: ₹600

There are no additional charges or taxes. All prices are final and clearly mentioned before checkout. Pricing is the same for all users.

Shipping Policy

This is a service-based website for booking library seats. There is no physical product or shipping involved. All confirmations and updates are sent digitally via email or SMS.

Terms and Conditions

This website is owned and managed by Chinmay Agrawal. By using this platform, users agree to the following terms:

  • Anyone can book seats in the library.
  • Bookings must be made in advance using the available time slots.
  • Users must adhere to library rules during their reserved time.
  • Misuse of the service or failure to follow rules may lead to cancellation of privileges.

Privacy Policy

We collect essential personal information such as name, email address, and mobile number to process bookings and send confirmations.

We do not store any payment details and do not share your personal information with any third party. This website is managed by Chinmay Agrawal, who ensures all user data is securely handled.

Cancellation/Refund Policy

Once a booking is confirmed and payment is successful, no cancellations or refunds will be allowed under any circumstances. Please review your booking details carefully before proceeding with payment.
